My favorite hobby

Skating

Hi!

Skating is my favorite hobby. I've been skating since I was 7. My favorite tricks on a scooter is a tail whip and a heel flip. My favorite trick on a skateboard is a kick flip and a chinese ollie. Skating is my favorite hobby because it lets me be what I want to and not get judged. Skating is a fun no experience hobby. I learnt skating on youtube and as I got better I went to real skateparks and tried the tricks I learnt. I've been learning for a good amount of time although I am not very good at skateboarding I don't give up. You will fall a lot as you try to learn but don't let that discourage you from being a master.
